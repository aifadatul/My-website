My Family

My family is my greatest source of strength and inspiration. They have always been my backbone, supporting every step and decision I make, especially in pursuing my dreams and academic goals. My friends also play a big role in brightening my days sharing life experiences, discovering new things together, and teaching me the true meaning of friendship and sincerity.

I come from a simple yet loving and supportive family. My father, Mr. Mohd Arif, is a strict but caring person. He has taught me the true meaning of responsibility, patience, and hard work in achieving success in life. My mother, Mrs. Ngakilah, is a gentle and kind-hearted woman who has always been my biggest source of encouragement, especially during challenging times in my studies and daily life.

I have six siblings, five sisters and one brother who always bring joy and laughter to our home. They are not only my companions but also my biggest supporters in everything I do. We share a very close bond, and although we sometimes have different opinions, the love between us never fades.

For me, family is the most precious gift in life. They are always by my side, giving me strength, advice, and unconditional love. Every achievement I have today would not be possible without their prayers and endless support.
