A Piece of My Story

Discover the friends and companions who have shaped my life and brought joy to my journey.

UiTM Friends

During my time at university, I was fortunate to meet several wonderful friends who have become very close to me. Our friendship began during orientation week and has remained strong until today. We spent a lot of time together completing assignments, sharing experiences as students, and supporting one another through both challenges and happy moments. Their presence made my university life more meaningful and filled with many unforgettable memories.

High School Friends

Before continuing my studies at university, I had six close friends whom I met during my high school years at SMK Cyberjaya. Our friendship began in 2020 and has remained strong until today, even though we are studying at the same university in different courses and at different locations. They have always been there for me through both difficult and happy times.

During semester breaks, we spend time together by going out for meals, watching movies, playing bowling, or simply chatting and laughing. Although we are all busy with our own responsibilities, our friendship remains strong. To me, they are not just friends, but like family.

My Cat

Taking care of him has taught me a lot about patience, responsibility, and love.
